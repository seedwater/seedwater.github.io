Our Vision

We envision a world where people care about each other, where people around the world have their basic needs met, where people are valued. We envision a world where humans respect and cherish the natural world, and serve as proud stewards of the resources and wonder of our planet. We envision a world where human goodness prevails over pain, suffering, and selfishness - where the morals of the many overcome the corruption of the few.

Seedwater Cafe can provide a space for people to learn, gather, have fun, and build a future they can believe in. The cafe can also remind us that ethical and mindful eating is for everyone, not just the privileged few.

Seedwater Solutions works towards a future of connection and partnership across national lines, empowering communities and individuals to lead us all into a more equitable and just future.

Seedwater Consulting provides rigorous and context-aware expertise and services to development projects around the world, and provides support to the other arms of the Seedwater Collective.
