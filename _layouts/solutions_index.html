WHO WE ARE

Seedwater Solutions is a mission-driven, human rights focused, development NGO with expertise in agriculture, policy, economic development, and public health. With experience in East and Southern Africa, Central America, Europe, and North America, we work to empower local communties to develop their capacity and deliver fundamental human rights in a self-directed and sustainable fashion.

WHAT WE DO

100% OF FUNDING TO PROJECTS, 100% CARBON OFFSET, FULL TRANSPARENCY

Non-profit organizations, non-governmental organizations, and other agencies need to collaborate, share best practices, and work together to our vision for the future. Seedwater is an NGO that believes in bridging disciplines and critically identifying, analyzing, and engineering the essential elements of multi-faceted community-led projects. We strongly believe in targeted, context-specific, community-driven and led development, and the principles of transparency, rigor, and an uncompromising pursuit of human rights.

WHERE WE WORK

Seedwater Consulting has worked in Kenya, Zambia, Rwanda, Ethiopia, and the U.S.A. - specifically in the state of Wisconsin. Our project involvement and leadership focuses in our areas of expertise: issues of agricultural development, capacity building, human health, and the environment. We work with local partners on the ground, international NGOs, governments, and individuals in need.

Justice-Driven Development

DEVELOPMENT /dɪˈvɛləpm(ə)nt/ (noun): a specified state of growth or advancement

ALL COUNTRIES ARE STILL DEVELOPING. The process of improvement and advancement is continual, as countries strive to achieve a more equitable, sustainable, and productive future. The trajectories of different communities, cultures, and nations will differ, with no strict path to peace and prosperity. However, Seedwater believes that true development cannot be achieved separately from the provision of fundamental human rights.

Seedwater is working to improve lives through sustainable provision of clean, affordable water.

Access to education is a a key component to unlocking the potential of the next generation.

Seedwater works with agriculture to build market-driven solutions pathways from poverty.

Supporting and empowering women and children is key for a just and joyful future.
